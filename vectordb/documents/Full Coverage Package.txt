4. Full Coverage Package
Description: The Full Coverage Package is designed for those who want complete protection. It combines liability, comprehensive, and collision coverage, ensuring you are protected in nearly every scenario.

Coverage Includes:

Liability: $100,000 per person and $300,000 per accident for bodily injury, and $50,000 for property damage.
Comprehensive: Includes all the benefits listed under the Comprehensive Coverage.
Collision: Includes all the benefits listed under the Collision Coverage.
Deductible: $500 for comprehensive and collision claims, providing a balance between coverage and out-of-pocket costs.

Premium: This is the most expensive option, but it is ideal for drivers seeking maximum security and peace of mind.
