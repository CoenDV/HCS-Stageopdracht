2. Comprehensive Coverage
Description: Comprehensive coverage safeguards against a variety of non-collision incidents. This includes theft, vandalism, fire, and damage from natural disasters like hail or flooding.

Coverage Includes:

Theft: Protection against loss of your vehicle due to theft.
Fire: Covers damage caused by fire or explosions.
Natural Disasters: Protection from damages resulting from events like hurricanes or tornadoes.
Animal Collisions: Covers damages incurred from hitting an animal, such as a deer.
Deductible: $500, meaning you would pay this amount out of pocket before the insurance kicks in.

Premium: This policy is moderately priced and is ideal for newer or high-value vehicles, providing peace of mind against various risks.
